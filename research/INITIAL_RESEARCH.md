## Important conclusions

- July is outside Maui's humpback-whale season. Do not sell a whale-watch expectation.
- Legal “pick your own fruit” opportunities are uncommon. ONO's reserved tour explicitly includes guided picking; elsewhere, buy from growers or ask permission.
- Hosmer Grove and the adjacent native shrubland are the practical public-access native bird target.
- Keālia is the closest high-value birding option, but many migrant species peak outside summer.
- Early morning is the default for calmer ocean conditions and lower heat, never a safety guarantee.
- A free or cheap stop can outrank an expensive tour when kid insight and logistics are stronger.
