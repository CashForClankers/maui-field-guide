# Creature field guide research

Checked June 27, 2026. The public page deliberately translates technical facts into short observations a child can test without approaching wildlife.

## Scientific sources

- **Honu / green sea turtle:** [NOAA Fisheries species science](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/species/green-turtle/science) for diet, identification, threats, and research; [NOAA Pacific Islands FAQ](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/pacific-islands/endangered-species-conservation/frequent-questions-sea-turtles-pacific-islands-region) for Hawaiʻi basking and the 10-foot viewing recommendation.
- **Lauʻīpala / yellow tang:** [University of Hawaiʻi Sea Grant interactive reef](https://seagrant.soest.hawaii.edu/?da_image=41729) for field marks, Hawaiian name, grazing role, and aquarium-trade context; [NOAA repository study](https://repository.library.noaa.gov/view/noaa/598) for habitat complexity, protected areas, and yellow-tang life stages.
- **Humuhumunukunukuāpuaʻa / reef triggerfish:** [University of Hawaiʻi Sea Grant interactive reef](https://seagrant.soest.hawaii.edu/?da_image=41729) for diet, commonness, state-fish context, and name; the photo record confirms `Rhinecanthus rectangulus` in Hawaiian reef habitat.
- **ʻĪlioholoikauaua / Hawaiian monk seal:** [NOAA Fisheries species profile](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/species/hawaiian-monk-seal) for endemism, population, diet, diving, and behavior; [NOAA viewing guidance](https://www.fisheries.noaa.gov/index.php/resource/outreach-materials/rule-thumb-viewing-hawaiian-monk-seals) for the 50-foot rule. Mothers with pups need 150 feet.
- **Aeʻo / Hawaiian stilt:** [USFWS Keālia Pond species guide](https://www.fws.gov/refuge/kealia-pond/species) for identification, shallow-water adaptation, breeding season, chick behavior, and disturbance risk.
- **ʻĀhihi-Kīnaʻu:** [Hawaiʻi DLNR reserve profile](https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/ecosystems/nars/maui/ahihi-kinau-2/) and [aquatic rules](https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dar/regulated-areas/ahihi-kinau-natural-area-reserve-maui/) for ecological significance and no-take protections.
